🍳 Re-seasoning my cast iron skillet with grapeseed oil. I'd been using avocado oil all this while, and the seasoning has not been built up because avocado oil has a high smoke point, and the 420 degrees F that I'd been baking the skillet at does not cause the oil to polymerize. Grapeseed oil has a smoke point that is close to that temperature, and the two coats I've added to the pan seem to be sticking well. Third time lucky - here goes another one.
